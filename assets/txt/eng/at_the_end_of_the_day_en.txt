At the end of the day

You understand that it is over.
and that energy passed through you.
That you understand, is only “that”.
So what brings you to write 
at the end of the day?
Life are poeting through you a framework.
The people pass through you unknowingly.
At the end of the day you retire from the framework.
Yet still stand in line.
Stand in line on the verge of gospel.
For thus operate life – inside order.
For thus operate life – alright.
For shapes and sounds and already
have we spoken about this before.
At the end of the day your bed is set to welcome you.
At the end of the day you are embraced in your own arms.
Your two arms, long lost lovers.
Not illusion of loves which is inside a daily framework.
Not illusion of fatherhood which is inside a daily framework.
At the end of the day it is you and your dear arms – at the end.
