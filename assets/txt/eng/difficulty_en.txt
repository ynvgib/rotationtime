Difficulty

Treading the winding path
they say that is the safe path
don't choose the easy trail
handle, persist, mature
all life is ahead of you
all ways are open
I divert that same
winding path
dealing, searching
the fortunate path
that which fits me,
is not curving
I choose the simple way
life without struggle,
life of growth
people of conflict speak
close their eyes 
to other things
