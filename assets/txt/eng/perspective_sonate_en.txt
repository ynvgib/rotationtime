Perspective Sonate

When I don’t wait, I take the bait
Then what I see, is what needs to be
and not what is, which brings no ease.
I see what I think I want here to transform
as I go farther from my form.
My Mind tries to solve the need, it pays no heed.
All to avoid mishap, I fall into a gap.
Busy in mental disarray, it’s a price I have to pay,
since I’m simply human, yet there’s another way.
In both, I have no choice, it’s luck
that either dumbs me as a duck
and puts me in life’s shooting range
or in response it brings the change.
Then all is left is reactive navigation. Right?
Observing survival fears and not what might…
