Write a Drawing

Don’t draw what you see
Don’t write what you hear
Don’t be and don’t linger in the open air
because everyone will come to devour you
in the sea
because everyone will come to devour you
at day
of your return home from the rough cruise 
deep in the municipal heart
of a rushing and gushing movement.

I am a poet as I am a seller of shirts
on the market to warm the hearts of the men who never came to shop
in my dukhan.
