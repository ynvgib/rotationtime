Reality 

18-10-2023

Eyes deep blue
ears poke aside
bolded hair, that is reality
acknowledge it and inhale into the lungs
it is the body within the soul
or the soul hidden in body
see no as it once was, a bit blur
rinkled skin, it is the age so they say
yellowed teeth, filling and holes
but there is a smile and all is normal
so why do they always count the age?
