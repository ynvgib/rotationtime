Appropriate Representation 

09-09-2023 23:12

Translated from Hebrew
Rhymed in Hebrew

I did not invent Yes and No
I did not discover Is Yot and Is Not
I heard much of bad and good
I treaded such back and forth again
I jumped up, I landed down
they asked me "Did you arrive?"
I wondered if to here or there
and inasmuch my clumsy manifested body
continued between this and that
to move in fermented life.
Outside fermented world, inside silent
but as I was picking
information pieces and recommendations
with working head, device lost in advice
peace was hiding well
it did not fear getting wet
from sea of tears not his
recognized surely the entirety
of a moment he could easily be unveiled
and smile a light headed smile
and be perceived oh-so docile
say to all "I know not"
and in a blink he uproots here
roots years old of lie
and present he the investigation
established on falls and rises
of ages of turmoils
and now peace sits content
awaits restrained
to speak of spirit wonders
and in between with smeared ink
exposes fears of paired element
which constitues my re-present-station.
