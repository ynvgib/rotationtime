Among Seasons

translated from hebrew
12-10-2023 9:29

you walk there
for you believe that there
it is.
When you have reached there
you found out that all the time
it is here, where you were.
Where you are now seeking
for the here, where you were,
is already there
and the here where you are now,
did not bring what you sought.
you wander in the garden
between here and there
and the seasons passing between lands.
you lean on a tree trunk
in one of the season 
fatigued from wandering between the lands,
between here and there.
you are confused and hear
a sound of a chirping bird.
That is all that was needed.
