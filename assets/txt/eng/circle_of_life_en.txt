Circle of Life

The Shapes are not always
   clear
The Time is ‘doing’ its thing
   invisibly
The People are taking time and shapes
   into reality
Illusion meets a dream for 100 years.
100 years of loneliness – written.
100 years of togetherness – told.

A man knows His Path Not – That is Life.
I was chosen like you to forget.
We are born to forget.
We are taught to remember shapes & sounds
for our Path in this Round.

 Circle of Life.
